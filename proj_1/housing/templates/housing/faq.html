Frequently Asked Questions

Below are common questions and answers about the NT Housing Maintenance System.

  1. How does this site run?
    Ans: The NT Housing Authority.
  2. How can I reset my password?
    Ans: Follow the reset password link on the Home page.
  3. Do you have warranty on jobs completed?
    Ans: Although all jobs have warranty, the warranty is for the Housing Authority and not the renter.
  4. Do I need to sign a contract?
    Ans: No, all contracts are between the tradesman and the NT Housing Authority.
  5. Do I pay anything when the tradesman comes to my home?
    Ans: No, all payments are between the Housing Authority and the tradesmen involved.
  6. How long will I wait for the repair to be completed?
    Ans: Although times vary, most urgent work is completed in a day while non-urgent standard jobs should be completed within a month from the original request.
